Bazooka Luca's 80/20 Mix

After several iterations and attempts to create the taste I am looking for in a hot sauce, I found solace in simplicity. I call it Bazooka Luca's 80/20-Mix.

Would you like to give it a try yourself? Here is my recipe:

80% Habaneros, 20% Carolina Reapers, no seeds, no pith
20% brine, ferment for two weeks
Mix and process in a mouli-légumes
80% chili mix + 20% white wine vinegar

Enjoy!

Bazooka Luca's 50/50 Mix

Just recently I discovered a second even simpler but even better recipe. I call it Bazooka Luca's 80/20-Mix.

Would you like to give it a try yourself? Here is my recipe:

100% Carolina Reapers, no seeds, no pith
30% brine, ferment for two months (good chance that the fermentation stopped after a couple of weeks already though)
Mix and process in a mouli-légumes
50% chili + 50% white wine vinegar

Enjoy!
